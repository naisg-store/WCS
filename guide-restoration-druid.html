Restoration Druid: Patch 12.0.7 Guide

The Meta Context

In World of Warcraft: Midnight (Patch 12.0.7), Restoration Druids remain the undisputed masters of proactive, heal-over-time (HoT) throughput. Empowered by the Keeper of the Grove Hero Talent tree, they gain immense synergistic value from summoning Grove Guardians that dynamically support their healing output and blanket the group in rejuvenating magic. Restoration shines in the heavy, sustained rot-damage encounters typical of the new Void-corrupted raids and Mythic+ dungeons. While they require intimate encounter knowledge to pre-ramp their healing effectively, their unparalleled mobility, robust utility (including Mark of the Wild and Rebirth), and potent spot-healing make them a top-tier choice for any endgame content.

Stat Priority

To maximize your healing throughput and mana efficiency in 12.0.7, focus on the following stat priority:

  • Intellect: Your primary stat. Always prioritize higher item level gear as Intellect directly scales the raw power of all your healing spells.
  • Haste: The most crucial secondary stat for Restoration. Haste lowers your global cooldown (GCD), reduces cast times, and crucially, increases the tick rate of all your Heal-over-Time (HoT) spells.
  • Mastery (Harmony): Immensely powerful for group and tank healing. Mastery increases your healing on a target for each of your HoT effects currently active on them, rewarding careful stacking of Lifebloom, Rejuvenation, and Wild Growth.
  • Versatility: Provides a flat increase to healing and damage, while offering vital damage reduction. Highly valuable for surviving high-tyrannical Mythic+ keys.
  • Critical Strike: Grants a chance for your heals to do double effect, but generally scales worse than Haste and Mastery for reliable, sustained throughput.

Core Rotation

Single-Target & Tank Healing

  1. Lifebloom Uptime: Maintain Lifebloom on the active tank (or yourself, depending on talent choices) at all times to trigger Clearcasting procs.
  2. Layer HoTs: Keep Rejuvenation active on targets taking sustained damage. Cast Cenarion Ward on cooldown for heavy tank damage.
  3. Grove Guardians: Summon a Treant (Keeper of the Grove) to provide potent, targeted Swiftmends and sustained Nourish healing on the primary target.
  4. Spot Healing: Use Swiftmend for immediate burst healing on dangerously low targets.
  5. Regrowth: Cast Regrowth as a filler heal, especially when Omen of Clarity (Clearcasting) procs for a free, mana-less cast.

AoE & Raid Healing

  1. Efflorescence: Maintain 100% uptime on Efflorescence under the largest cluster of melee players or the stacked ranged group.
  2. Pre-Ramp HoTs: Anticipate incoming group damage by blanketing the party/raid with Rejuvenation 5-10 seconds before the damage event.
  3. Wild Growth: Cast Wild Growth immediately as the group takes AoE damage to stabilize health pools.
  4. Major Cooldowns: Use Tranquility for massive, channeled raid-wide healing during critical damage phases. Pop Incarnation: Tree of Life / Convoke the Spirits for extended periods of intense group damage.
  5. Flourish: Cast Flourish immediately after applying your major AoE HoTs (Wild Growth and multiple Rejuvenations) to extend their duration and boost their tick rate.

Consumables & Enhancements

Ensure you are fully prepared for progression with the optimal Patch 12.0.7 consumables:

  • Flask: Flask of the Whispering Woods (Grants a massive Intellect boost with a chance to restore mana on spell cast).
  • Potion: Potion of Channeled Mending for intense mana recovery, or Potion of Void-Touched Prowess for burst healing windows.
  • Food: Grand Void-Seared Feast (Intellect) to buff the entire group.
  • Weapon Enchant: Enchant Weapon - Verdant Grace (Procs extra healing and Intellect).
  • Gems: Socket your gear with Quick Void-Emerald to heavily stack Haste.
