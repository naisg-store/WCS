Affliction Warlock: Patch 12.0.7 Guide

The Meta Context

In Patch 12.0.7 of the Midnight expansion, Affliction Warlock stands out as a premiere choice for sustained multi-target spread and council-style fights, while holding formidable ground in high-tier Mythic+ settings. Its identity revolves around meticulous damage-over-time management and rapid Soul Shard expenditure. The specification achieves peak efficiency by leaning into its Hero Talent options: Soul Harvester accelerates passive execute profiles and dark damage amplification, whereas Hellcaller leans aggressively into burning targets down with fast-ticking global curses. Affliction remains highly sought after in raiding setups thanks to fundamental group utility like Demonic Gateway, Healthstones, and Soulstones.

Stat Priority

Correct secondary stat distribution ensures that your multi-DoT layouts tick rapidly while scaling up the strength of your primary Soul Shard spenders.

  • Intellect: Your primary stat. Directly increases the base spell power and overall damage value of all offensive spells in your repertoire. Prioritize this by seeking higher item-level gear updates.
  • Haste: Reduces global cooldowns, accelerates long casting animations, and increases the tick frequency of your core damage-over-time spells. High Haste values are crucial for smooth Soul Shard generation.
  • Mastery (Mastery: Potent Afflictions): Directly boosts the overall damage dealt by your primary damage-over-time spells, including Agony, Corruption, Unstable Affliction, and Wither.
  • Critical Strike: Provides your damage spells and tick events a standard chance to hit for double damage, while interacting with passive procs that reward periodic critical strikes.
  • Versatility: Offers a flat percentage damage increase and reduces incoming damage taken. Highly useful for defensive scaling in higher Mythic+ keystones where survival is crucial.

Core Rotation

Affliction gameplay centers around ensuring maximum up-time on your primary dots, generating Soul Shards through Agony ticks, and bursting targets down with Malefic Rapture loops.

Single-Target Combat Priority

  1. Apply and strictly maintain Unstable Affliction on the primary target.
  2. Apply and maintain Agony to ensure consistent Soul Shard generation.
  3. Maintain Corruption (or Wither, depending on your talent setup) on your target at all times.
  4. Cast Summon Darkglare during major burst windows to extend the duration of active damage-over-time effects.
  5. Cast Phantom Singularity (or Vile Taint) on cooldown to apply massive background damage pressure.
  6. Spend your Soul Shards on Malefic Rapture to deal explosive damage based on the total number of dots active on the target. Do not allow your Soul Shards to cap at maximum.
  7. Channel Shadow Bolt (or Drain Soul) as your primary resource filler spell when all dots are refreshed and shards are spent.

AoE & Multi-Target Priority

  1. Cast Vile Taint to instantly apply Agony and Curse of Agony across tightly packed groups of enemies.
  2. Manually apply Unstable Affliction to the high-priority target within the enemy cluster.
  3. Ensure Corruption / Wither is active across all secondary threats.
  4. Activate your major cooldown via Summon Darkglare when groups are fully dotted up.
  5. Chain cast Malefic Rapture consecutively to chunk down the entire pull simultaneously through dot detonation.
  6. Fill open casting gaps with Seed of Corruption or broad channeling blocks to maintain baseline multi-target cleave.

Consumables & Enhancements

Secure top-tier performance benchmarks in Patch 12.0.7 by configuring your Affliction Warlock with the correct endgame enhancements.

  • Flask: Flask of Alchemical Chaos (or an equivalent pure Intellect option).
  • Potion: Tempered Potion for massive primary stat spikes, or Algari Burst Potion during coordinated raid bloodlust phases.
  • Food Buffet: Feast of the Divine Day for the maximum baseline Intellect increase.
  • Weapon Enchant: Enchant Weapon - Authority of Radiant Power.
  • Gems & Ring Enchants: Radiant Haste or Radiant Mastery combinations tailored to your preferred stat goals.
