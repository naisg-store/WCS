Windwalker Monk: Patch 12.0.7 Guide

The Meta Context

In Patch 12.0.7, Windwalker Monks maintain their status as highly mobile melee powerhouses, excelling in high-mobility encounters and sustained AoE scenarios. Through the refined synergy of the Shado-Pan and Conduit of the Celestials Hero Talent trees, Windwalkers can dish out immense burst windows during their cooldown phases, while remaining consistently effective in Mythic+ dungeons and raid environments.

Stat Priority

  • Agility: Your primary stat; always favor items with higher item levels to boost your overall damage.
  • Versatility: The most consistent damage multiplier for your entire toolkit while providing crucial damage reduction.
  • Critical Strike: Essential for scaling your heavy-hitting abilities like Rising Sun Kick and Fists of Fury.
  • Haste: Reduces global cooldowns and energy regeneration time, allowing for a smoother, faster-paced rotation.
  • Mastery: Increases the value of your Combo Strikes passive, rewarding varied ability sequences.

Core Rotation

Single-Target Priority

  1. Activate Storm, Earth, and Fire to maximize damage output.
  2. Cast Rising Sun Kick whenever available to reset cooldowns and maintain pressure.
  3. Use Fists of Fury to channel significant damage into the target.
  4. Fill with Blackout Kick and Tiger Palm to manage energy and maintain the Combo Strikes buff.

AoE/Cleave Priority

  1. Apply Mark of the Crane by spinning effectively.
  2. Use Whirling Dragon Punch or Spinning Crane Kick to distribute damage across all enemies.
  3. Maintain Rising Sun Kick on the primary target to ensure pressure.
  4. Utilize Storm, Earth, and Fire for massive spread pressure on larger packs.

Consumables & Enhancements

  • Flasks: Flask of Alchemical Chaos (or appropriate primary stat flask).
  • Potions: Potion of Spectral Agility for high-priority burst windows.
  • Food: Well-fed buff providing Agility and Versatility.
  • Weapon Enchants: Enchant Weapon - Sophic Devotion for reliable Agility procs.
