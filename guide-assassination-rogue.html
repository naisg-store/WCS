Assassination Rogue: Patch 12.0.7 Guide

The Meta Context

In Patch 12.0.7 of Midnight, Assassination Rogues excel as a premier Melee DPS[cite: 2] option known for devastating single-target burst and exceptional priority funnel damage. With the current tuning of Hero Talent trees, the Deathstalker build has emerged as the definitive choice for both progression raiding and high Tyrannical Mythic+ keys. By leveraging the Deathstalker's Mark to amplify damage against primary targets, Assassination players can systematically execute high-health enemies while maintaining a steady stream of lethal poisons and bleeding effects on secondary targets.

Stat Priority

For an Assassination Rogue, maximizing the potency of your damage-over-time effects and poison applications dictates your gear choices:

  • Agility: Your primary stat, which directly increases your Attack Power and the base damage of all abilities and bleeds.
  • Mastery (Potent Assassin): Substantially increases the damage of your poisons and bleeds. Because a massive portion of Assassination's damage profile comes from these effects, Mastery is your most valuable secondary stat.
  • Critical Strike: Increases the chance for your abilities and DoTs to critically hit, synergizing well with the Seal Fate passive to generate additional combo points.
  • Versatility: Provides a straightforward flat damage increase and damage mitigation, which is highly prized for survivability in high-end content.
  • Haste: Increases energy regeneration and attack speed (meaning more poison procs), but generally scales less efficiently than Mastery and Crit for burst windows.

Core Rotation

The Assassination rotation is a strict priority system centered around maintaining bleeds, managing energy, and capitalizing on the Deathmark cooldown window.

Single-Target Priority

  • Maintain Slice and Dice at all times for increased attack speed and poison application.
  • Apply and maintain Garrote and Rupture on your target. Never let these bleeds drop.
  • Cast Deathmark on cooldown, ideally overlapping with your on-use trinkets, potions, and maximum combo points for a massive burst phase.
  • Use Shiv on cooldown to amplify your Nature (poison) damage against the target for 8 seconds.
  • Cast Mutilate (or Ambush if procced/stealthed) to generate combo points.
  • Spend your combo points (at 5 or more) on Envenom to deal instant poison damage and increase your poison application chance.

AoE Priority

  • Maintain Garrote on as many targets as possible (up to 3 or 4) to benefit from the energy regeneration of the Venomous Wounds passive.
  • Apply Rupture to multiple long-lived targets.
  • Cast Crimson Tempest to apply an AoE bleed to the entire pack.
  • Use Fan of Knives as your primary combo point generator when facing 3 or more stacked enemies.
  • Cycle Envenom on your primary target to maintain the Envenom buff while your AoE bleeds tick.

Consumables & Enhancements

Optimize your performance in Patch 12.0.7 with the following endgame enhancements:

  • Gems: Socket one Agile Eversong Diamond as your primary meta gem. Fill your remaining sockets with Flawless Quick Amethyst or Flawless Deadly Onyx to maximize your Mastery and Critical Strike.
  • Flasks & Potions: Utilize the Flask of Alchemical Chaos or the top-tier Agility flask of the Midnight expansion. Carry maximum-quality Potions of Unhindered Strikes for your Deathmark burn phases.
  • Food & Enchants: Consume the Midnight primary Agility feast or personal Mastery food. Ensure your daggers are enchanted with the highest tier Midnight Agility and Mastery enchants available.
