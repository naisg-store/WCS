Unholy Death Knight: Patch 12.0.7 Guide

The Meta Context

Unholy Death Knight in Patch 12.0.7 (Midnight) is a powerhouse Melee DPS, excelling in both single-target and burst AoE scenarios. The current meta heavily favors the Rider of the Apocalypse Hero Talent tree. This tree summons the legendary Four Horsemen to assist you in combat, providing massive burst windows, incredible utility, and sustained pressure. Paired with Apex Talents like Forbidden Knowledge, Unholy dominates Mythic+ and Raid progression by turning Death Coil into the devastating Necrotic Coil and unleashing an endless army of undead.

Stat Priority

The optimal stat breakdown for an Unholy Death Knight is: Strength > Mastery ≥ Critical Strike > Haste > Versatility

  • Strength: Your primary stat. It is a straightforward throughput increase to every aspect of your damage, scaling both your abilities and your minions.
  • Mastery: Increases your Shadow damage and the damage of your Minions. Because the vast majority of Unholy's damage profile is shadow or pet-based, this stat scales exceptionally well in both single-target and AoE situations.
  • Critical Strike: Synergizes directly with Mastery and various class talents (such as Brittle) that increase critical strike damage, making it your highest priority secondary stat after Mastery.
  • Haste: Lowers the global cooldown and increases attack speed, but does not affect your disease tick rate, placing its value significantly lower than Mastery and Crit.
  • Versatility: Provides a flat damage increase and damage reduction. It gains value later in the expansion due to diminishing returns on other stats, but remains the lowest priority early on.

Core Rotation

Single-Target Priority

  1. Cast Army of the Dead alongside Dark Transformation (ideally macroed together) to trigger Commander of the Dead and summon your Rider of the Apocalypse.
  2. Apply and maintain Virulent Plague on your primary target.
  3. Cast Scourge Strike (or Vampiric Strike during Dark Transformation) to build Lesser Ghoul stacks and spend Runes.
  4. Consume Sudden Doom procs and excess Runic Power with Death Coil (or Necrotic Coil).
  5. Cast Festering Strike to apply Festering Wounds if the target is entirely missing them.

AoE Priority

  1. Cast Army of the Dead and Dark Transformation to unleash your empowered minions and enable sweeping claws for massive cleave.
  2. Maintain Virulent Plague across all targets.
  3. Cast Death and Decay to enable Scourge Strike to cleave up to 5 additional targets.
  4. Spam Scourge Strike while inside Death and Decay to burst AoE damage and apply/burst wounds.
  5. Consume Sudden Doom procs and excess Runic Power with Epidemic (or Graveyard) instead of Death Coil.

Consumables & Enhancements

  • Flasks: Flask of the Shattered Sun
  • Potions: Potion of Recklessness (Combat) and Silvermoon Health Potion (Healing)
  • Food: Harandar Celebration (Feast) or Royal Roast
  • Weapon Enchant: Rune of the Apocalypse
  • Weapon Oil: Thalassian Phoenix Oil
  • Gems: Dual-stat Amethyst gems (e.g., Flawless Deadly Amethyst) to heavily prioritize Mastery and Crit.
