Retribution Paladin: Patch 12.0.7 Guide

The Meta Context

In Patch 12.0.7 of the Midnight expansion, Retribution Paladin shines as an incredibly secure, heavy burst melee DPS with exceptional mid-range adaptability and unmatched survivability features. By mastering Holy Power production and spending resources on high-impact holy spenders, the spec maintains a powerful presence in endgame raid tiers and mythic dungeons alike. The mechanical depth of the meta shifts gracefully between its distinctive Hero Talent branches: Herald of the Sun integrates beautiful, blazing solar burn over-time layers that erupt during cooldown tracking, while Templar focuses on unleashing massive physical and holy strikes that drop skyward hammers down on top of active pulls. Retribution remains a mandatory asset to community compositions due to cornerstone utilities like Blessing of Protection, Lay on Hands, and Devotion Aura.

Stat Priority

Balancing your secondary stat configurations ensures that your holy resource engine cycles continuously without dropping output pressure.

  • Strength: Your primary stat baseline. Directly scales the raw attack power, ability scaling multipliers, and absolute baseline impact of every single blade and hammer spell in your arsenal.
  • Haste: Accelerates your weapon swing timer, shortens the universal global cooldown, and reduces the recovery timers of your core holy generators, ensuring smooth Holy Power velocity.
  • Critical Strike: Grants your physical strikes and holy spenders a static chance to hit for double damage, pairing cleanly with passive reset modifiers.
  • Mastery (Mastery: Highlord's Judgment): Directly increases the flat percentage damage bonus applied to all of your Holy damage capabilities, making your primary finishers hit significantly harder.
  • Versatility: Offers a flat percentage increase to all outgoing damage vectors while directly reducing all incoming damage taken, providing an excellent defensive floor for high-tier Mythic+ dungeons.

Core Rotation

Retribution gameplay centers around generating Holy Power cleanly up to limits through baseline strikes, and utilizing finishers to execute high priority damage cycles.

Single-Target Combat Priority

  1. Cast Avenging Wrath (or Crusade) strictly on cooldown during major group burst windows.
  2. Spend Holy Power on Final Verdict (or Templar's Verdict) whenever you hold 4 or more Holy Power to prevent resource capping.
  3. Cast Wake of Ashes on cooldown when resource pools are empty to instantly max out Holy Power and trigger burst procs.
  4. Execute Blade of Justice on cooldown to generate Holy Power and apply active damage-over-time traits.
  5. Cast Judgment strictly on cooldown to generate Holy Power and apply the active target damage vulnerability modifier.
  6. Use Hammer of Wrath whenever it becomes active via procs or during target execute phases (below 20% health).
  7. Use Crusader Strike as your basic active resource filler when all secondary generators are cooling down.

AoE & Multi-Target Priority

  1. Cast Wake of Ashes early into the center of the enemy cluster to generate full Holy Power instantly.
  2. Spend Holy Power pools strictly on Divine Storm, sending out swirling waves of holy energy across all tightly clustered threats.
  3. Use Judgment with talented multi-target jumping attributes to spread active vulnerability tags across the pack.
  4. Execute Blade of Justice procs, exploiting talented tracking traits that cause the holy blades to strike multiple targets concurrently.
  5. Maintain regular resource production cycles to guarantee a non-stop barrage of Divine Storm actions.

Consumables & Enhancements

Maximize your damage metrics in Patch 12.0.7 by optimizing your equipment layouts with these recommended additions.

  • Flask: Flask of Alchemical Chaos (or an equivalent pure Strength alternative).
  • Potion: Tempered Potion for stat surges, or Algari Burst Potion aligned with bloodlust mechanics.
  • Food Buffet: Feast of the Divine Day for a heavy Strength baseline increase.
  • Weapon Enchant: Enchant Weapon - Authority of Radiant Power.
  • Gems & Ring Enchants: Radiant Haste or Radiant Mastery configurations tuned to hit your chosen pacing thresholds.
