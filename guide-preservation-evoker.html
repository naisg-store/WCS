Preservation Evoker: Patch 12.0.7 Guide

The Meta Context

In Patch 12.0.7, Preservation Evoker remains a premier healer, highly valued for its exceptional burst healing and utility within raid and Mythic+ environments. Leveraging the synergy between the Chronowarden and Scalecommander Hero Talent trees, Evokers excel at manipulating time and space to stabilize the party during high-damage windows. Mastery of "Empower" spells and positioning is essential to fully realize the class's potential as a reactive and proactive healer.

Stat Priority

  • Intellect: Primary stat; maximizes raw healing and spell power.
  • Mastery (Life-Binder): The most important secondary stat, as it increases healing effectiveness based on the target's current health, making you incredibly efficient at "topping off" allies.
  • Haste: Reduces the cast time of your Empower spells and lowers global cooldowns, crucial for weaving fast reactions into your rotation.
  • Critical Strike: Provides valuable burst potential for your primary heals.
  • Versatility: Offers necessary damage reduction for self-sustain and consistent healing throughput.

Core Rotation

Single-Target Priority

  1. Keep Echo active on high-priority targets to duplicate your next spell.
  2. Use Reversion on cooldown to maintain consistent, passive healing.
  3. Empower Dream Breath and Spiritbloom to handle major spikes in damage.
  4. Cast Living Flame to heal or generate Essence if you need to stabilize the target.

AoE/Raid Healing Priority

  1. Spread Echo across the raid using Emerald Communion or multiple casts to prepare for burst.
  2. Empower Dream Breath to its maximum rank for massive group-wide HoT coverage.
  3. Use Rewind to undo significant raid-wide damage spikes.
  4. Deploy Stasis to store your healing spells and release them simultaneously during high-pressure mechanics.

Consumables & Enhancements

  • Flasks: Flask of Alchemical Chaos.
  • Potions: Potion of Withering Vitality for burst healing; Refreshing Healing Potion for personal survival.
  • Food: Grand Banquet of the Kalu'ak (or equivalent Intellect/Mastery food).
  • Weapon Enchants: Enchant Weapon - Sophic Devotion.
