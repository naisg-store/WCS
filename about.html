About

The Origin Story

If you are anything like me, you love this game but don't always have the time to read a ten-page dissertation just to figure out what stats to prioritize on your latest alt. I’ve been running around Azeroth since the original Friends and Family Alpha, dominating the meters through Classic and The Burning Crusade. But jumping back into modern Retail to level alts? The sheer volume of constantly shifting systems, dynamic stat weights, and complex spell priorities is enough to make anyone's head spin.

I originally built this tool to solve my own problem. Traditional guide sites are fantastic resources for top-tier mythic raiders, but for the average returning player, they can be slow, bogged down by heavy advertisements, and incredibly dense. When you are standing in front of a boss or quickly trying to buy consumables off the Auction House, you don't need an essay—you need an answer.

The Tech Behind the Tool

Building clean, efficient, and user-centric web tools is what I do. With a professional background operating as an IT systems engineer, web designer, and SEO specialist, I wanted to apply modern web architecture to World of Warcraft data. My history with WoW's backend systems goes all the way back to the original Vanilla release, tinkering with LUA code and developing custom UI addons to streamline the gameplay experience.

Today, this site serves as a spiritual successor to those early addon days. It strips away the bloat and focuses entirely on performance and readability.

How the Data Works

To ensure the information here is as accurate as it is fast, the Midnight Cheat Sheet leverages automated, data-driven workflows. Rather than relying on static, opinion-based guides that become outdated weeks after a patch drops, this tool aggregates the most current SimulationCraft data and top-tier community meta builds for Patch 12.0.7.

The raw data is then parsed, condensed, and delivered through a lightweight, dynamically fetched interface. The result is a lightning-fast, ad-free resource that gives you exactly what you need to know about your class's single-target rotation, AoE priorities, major cooldowns, and standard consumables at a single glance.

Whether you are a returning veteran or just trying out a new specialization for the first time, this cheat sheet is built to keep you out of your browser and in the game.
