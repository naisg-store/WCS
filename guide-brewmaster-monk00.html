Brewmaster Monk: Patch 12.0.7 Guide

The Meta Context

In Patch 12.0.7, Brewmaster Monk remains the premier choice for players who value high mobility, exceptional utility, and the most consistent damage intake profile in the game. With the refinement of the Master of Harmony and Shado-Pan Hero Talent trees, Brewmasters have gained significant passive durability and burst threat generation. In Mythic+, their ability to kite while maintaining high stagger uptime makes them invaluable for high-key progression, while their raid presence is solidified by strong external cooldowns and unparalleled survivability during physical damage spikes.

Stat Priority

For Brewmaster, consistency is key to surviving high-pressure situations. Aim for the following priority:

  • Agility: Your primary source of Attack Power and Armor; always prioritize the highest item level gear.
  • Haste: Essential for reducing the cooldown of Keg Smash and Blackout Kick, increasing your Brew generation for Purifying Brew.
  • Crit: Increases damage and provides extra procs for Celestial Brew uptime.
  • Versatility: Provides flat damage reduction and increased output, smoothing out your damage intake.
  • Mastery: Increases your chance to dodge attacks; valuable, but lower priority than Haste and Crit.

Core Rotation

The Brewmaster rotation centers on managing your resources (Energy and Brew charges) to maintain high uptime on defensive buffs.

Single-Target Priority

  1. Use Keg Smash on cooldown to generate Brew charges and energy.
  2. Cast Tiger Palm to reduce the remaining cooldown of your Brews.
  3. Maintain Blackout Kick for the defensive buff.
  4. Use Purifying Brew to clear your Stagger damage when the bar turns into a dangerous red color.
  5. Use Celestial Brew to mitigate incoming spike damage.

AoE Priority

  1. Keg Smash: Use on cooldown to tag all enemies and maximize threat.
  2. Breath of Fire: Keep this debuff active on all targets for damage reduction.
  3. Spinning Crane Kick: Use when you have excess energy to maintain threat and damage.
  4. Invoke Niuzao, the Black Ox: Use for major packs to handle burst damage.

Consumables & Enhancements

  • Flask: Flask of Alchemical Chaos.
  • Potion: Potion of Unbridled Fury (for threat) or Potion of Withering Vitality (for survival).
  • Food: Feast of the Midnight Banquet (or stat food boosting Haste/Versatility).
  • Weapon Enchant: Enchant Weapon - Authority of the Depths.
