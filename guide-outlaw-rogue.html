Outlaw Rogue: Patch 12.0.7 Guide

The Meta Context

In Patch 12.0.7 of Midnight, Outlaw Rogues maintain a strong presence as a highly mobile and resilient Melee DPS in both Mythic+ and raid environments. The current endgame meta heavily favors the Fatebound Hero Talent tree, which empowers the spec through Fatebound Coin flips; every 5+ combo point finisher has a chance to flip a coin, drastically stacking your damage output with consecutive successful flips. Furthermore, the introduction of Midnight's Apex Talents has unlocked "Gravedigger" for Outlaws, causing Between the Eyes to apply potent damage buffs and enhancing Dispatch to deal additional physical damage. Successfully piloting the spec requires adapting on the fly to your Roll the Bones buffs while keeping your Adrenaline Rush uptime as close to 100% as possible.

Stat Priority

For an Outlaw Rogue, your stats dictate your energy regeneration speed and the sheer physical power of your finishers:

  • Agility: Your core primary stat, directly scaling your Attack Power and the baseline damage of all abilities.
  • Versatility: Exceptionally valuable for Outlaw. It provides a reliable flat damage multiplier and significant damage reduction, which is vital for surviving high-tier Mythic+ mechanics.
  • Critical Strike: Boosts the chance for your abilities to deal double damage. It synergizes heavily with the guaranteed critical strikes and critical damage modifiers associated with Between the Eyes.
  • Haste: Increases energy regeneration and attack speed, allowing for faster combo point generation and smoother overall gameplay.
  • Mastery (Main Gauche): Generally considered the weakest stat for Outlaw, as it only increases the chance of secondary off-hand strikes and offers poor scaling compared to Versatility and Critical Strike.

Core Rotation

Outlaw is a rapid, reaction-heavy specialization that revolves around maintaining crucial buffs and maximizing the throughput of your cooldowns.

Single-Target Priority

  • Maintain Slice and Dice at all times to ensure maximum attack speed.
  • Cast Adrenaline Rush on cooldown to massively increase energy regeneration and attack speed, while reducing your global cooldown from 1 second down to 0.8 seconds.
  • Manage your Roll the Bones buffs meticulously. Always reroll if you secure a weak, single buff. If you land high-value buffs (like Broadside or Ruthless Precision) or a multi-buff stage, cast Keep It Rolling to extend their duration by an additional 30 seconds.
  • Use Between the Eyes on cooldown when at 5 or more combo points to trigger your Gravedigger Apex talent buffs.
  • Cast Dispatch as your primary finisher at 5+ combo points to deal heavy physical damage and trigger your Fatebound Coin flips.
  • Spam Sinister Strike to generate combo points, substituting with Pistol Shot whenever you receive an Opportunity proc.

AoE Priority

  • Activate Blade Flurry before casting any offensive abilities. This is non-negotiable, as it allows your single-target rotation to cleave onto all nearby targets.
  • Maintain your core single-target priorities—Adrenaline Rush, Roll the Bones, and Slice and Dice—while Blade Flurry is active.
  • Target the highest health enemy in the pack with Between the Eyes to secure the critical damage debuff.
  • Rapidly build combo points and unleash Dispatch to execute massive, consecutive AoE cleave damage.
  • Use Blade Rush (if talented) during heavy AoE pulls for immediate burst damage and rapid energy regeneration.

Consumables & Enhancements

Optimize your Outlaw Rogue for Patch 12.0.7 endgame content with these recommendations:

  • Gems: Equip one Agile Eversong Diamond as your primary meta gem. Fill your remaining sockets with Flawless Deadly Onyx (Versatility/Critical Strike) to maximize your high-priority secondary stats.
  • Flasks & Potions: Utilize the Midnight-tier primary Agility flask, or the Flask of Alchemical Chaos if optimizing for sheer secondary stats. Keep high-quality combat potions ready to pair with your Adrenaline Rush and Bloodlust windows.
  • Food & Enchants: Consume the Hollandar Celebration Feast for the highest possible stat gains during group content. Apply Phoenix Oil to your weapons to continuously empower your secondary stats in combat.
