Frost Death Knight: Patch 12.0.7 Guide

The Meta Context: Relentless Ice

In World of Warcraft: Midnight, Frost Death Knights deliver simple, high-impact shattering burst options. Built around tracking Killing Machine procs and crushing priority targets beneath heavy frost damage, the spec thrives in rapid-fire engagements. Patch 12.0.7 favors two core build routes: the iconic **Breath of Sindragosa** window for extended burst windows, or the heavily favored **Obliteration** build utilizing dual-wielding speed to pop massive Obliterates back-to-back.

Stat Analysis: Piercing Cold

For Patch 12.0.7, scaling your baseline Obliterate crits and spellpower throughput means adjusting your stats cleanly:

Strength > Mastery > Critical Strike > Haste > Versatility

  • Strength scales all weapon swings, baseline abilities, and general attack power.
  • Mastery (Frozen Heart) increases your total Frost damage output, which scales every key damage dealer in your rotation.
  • Critical Strike is vital for generating Killing Machine procs naturally during window down-times.
  • Haste speeds up resource flows and Rune generation to avoid dead spots in the rotation.

Core Rotation Nuance

The gameplay loop centers entirely on keeping your diseases rolling, building runic resources, and expending procs efficiently.

Single-Target Priority (Obliteration Build)

  • Apply and maintain Remorseless Winter on cooldown to keep passive cleave active.
  • Cast Pillar of Frost immediately on cooldown to initiate your primary execution window.
  • Consume Killing Machine procs instantly on Obliterate.
  • Spend Runic Power on Frost Strike whenever you lack procs to activate dynamic runic returns.
  • Use Howling Blast exclusively when Rime procs are active to deal free ranged frost damage.

Consumables & Enhancements

  • Flask: Flask of Alchemical Chaos or Flask of Tempered Mastery.
  • Weapon Buff: Rune of the Fallen Crusader (Main Hand) / Rune of Hysteria or Razorice (Off-Hand).
  • Ring Enchants: Devotion of Mastery.
  • Food: Feast of Divine Appetites.
