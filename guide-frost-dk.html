Frost Death Knight: Patch 12.0.7 Guide

The Meta Context

Frost Death Knight in Patch 12.0.7 (Midnight) is a burst-heavy Melee DPS defined by short, explosive burst windows synchronized with Pillar of Frost, a 45-second cooldown that amplifies your Strength by 30%. The current meta sees Frost performing exceptionally well in both Mythic+ and Raids when utilizing the 2H Breath of Sindragosa build paired with the Deathbringer Hero Talent tree. The addition of Midnight's Apex Talent system, specifically "Chosen of the Frostbrood," further escalates your burst potential by extending your power capabilities within these crucial windows.

Stat Priority

The optimal stat priority heavily depends on your specific build, but the general breakdown for the popular Deathbringer Breath of Sindragosa build is: Critical Strike > Mastery > Haste > Versatility.

  • Critical Strike: Buffs your overall damage output and increases the chance of generating Killing Machine procs, which are vital for your rotation.
  • Mastery: Directly boosts your overall Frost damage output, scaling extremely well for AoE and cleave scenarios.
  • Haste: Speeds up your Global Cooldowns (GCD) and increases your rune recharge rate.
  • Versatility: Provides a flat damage increase and damage reduction, though it is typically the lowest priority.

Core Rotation

Single-Target Priority

  1. Use Pillar of Frost, Reaper's Mark, and Frostwyrm's Fury together as your core 45-second burst window.
  2. Maintain Frost Fever on the target by using Howling Blast.
  3. Cast Obliterate when you have a Killing Machine proc. Never let Killing Machine overcap to two stacks.
  4. Cast Howling Blast when you have a Rime proc.
  5. Use Frost Strike to spend excess Runic Power and prevent capping.

AoE Priority

  1. Use Remorseless Winter on cooldown to establish your AoE presence.
  2. Maintain 5 stacks of Razorice on your primary target.
  3. At 3 or more targets, replace Obliterate with Frostscythe as your main rune spender.
  4. At 3 or more targets, replace Frost Strike with Glacial Advance as your main Runic Power spender.
  5. Cast Howling Blast with a Rime proc to spread cleave damage.

Consumables & Enhancements

  • Flask: Flask of the Shattered Sun
  • Potion: Potential of Light (Combat) and Silvermoon Healing Potion (Healing)
  • Food: Royal Roast
  • Weapon Enchants: Rune of the Fallen Crusader (Main-hand) and Rune of Razorice (Off-hand if dual-wielding)
  • Weapon Oil: Thalassian Phoenix Oil
  • Augment Rune: Void-Marked Augment Rune
