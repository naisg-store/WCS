Brewmaster Monk: Patch 12.0.7 Guide

The Meta Context

In Patch 12.0.7, Brewmaster Monks remain the premier "smooth damage" tanks, relying on their unique Stagger mechanic to nullify burst windows that would instantly kill other tank specializations. With current tuning, Brewmasters are highly sought after in high-tier Mythic+ keys and raids due to their massive toolkit of utility, including Ring of Peace, Leg Sweep, and increased mobility. Focus on the Master of Harmony Hero Talent tree to maximize your defensive synergy with Effuse and Celestial Brew, significantly bolstering your physical damage mitigation.

Stat Priority

  • Agility: Your primary source of Attack Power and Armor. Always prioritize item level.
  • Haste: Essential for reducing cooldowns on Keg Smash and Breath of Fire, ensuring higher uptime on Ironskin Brew.
  • Versatility: Increases damage dealt and provides passive damage reduction. Vital for overall survivability.
  • Critical Strike: Procs Celestial Fortune for additional healing and increases overall threat generation.
  • Mastery: Increases your dodge chance and provides a powerful Attack Power boost.

Core Rotation

Your goal is to maintain near-100% uptime on Ironskin Brew while keeping Purifying Brew active to cleanse your Stagger bar.

Single-Target Priority

  1. Keg Smash: Use on cooldown to generate energy and reset cooldowns.
  2. Breath of Fire: Maintain the debuff on your primary target for physical damage reduction.
  3. Tiger Palm: Spend excess energy and reduce the cooldown of Brews.
  4. Blackout Kick: Use to maintain the Blackout Combo buff.

AoE Combat Priority

  1. Keg Smash: Highest priority to keep aggro on the pack.
  2. Breath of Fire: Apply to all enemies for passive mitigation.
  3. Spinning Crane Kick: Use when multiple targets are present to maintain high threat.
  4. Rushing Jade Wind: Keep active for consistent pulsing AoE threat.

Consumables & Enhancements

  • Flask: Flask of Alchemical Chaos.
  • Weapon Enchant: Enchant Weapon - Authority of the Depths.
  • Food: Feast of the Midnight Banquet for primary stats.
  • Potions: Potion of Withering Dreams for high-pressure situations.
