Augmentation Evoker: Patch 12.0.7 Guide

The Meta Context: Temporal Empowerment

In World of Warcraft: Midnight, Augmentation Evokers stand out as the definitive force multiplier for high-end raiding and Mythic+ keys. Funneling raw Bronze and Black dragonflight power directly into your group, Augmentation turns standard damage profiles into lethal meta execution. Patch 12.0.7 places them firmly in the S-Tier, emphasizing extended **Ebon Might** windows. Mastering this spec means shifting focus away from personal combat charts to maintaining flawless buff tracking on your highest-performing damage allies.

Stat Analysis: Force Multiplication

To ensure your baseline buff windows scale properly and transfer maximum primary attributes to your allies, prioritize this distribution:

Intellect > Mastery > Haste > Critical Strike > Versatility

  • Intellect: Directly anchors the baseline potency value of Ebon Might attribute hand-offs.
  • Mastery (Timewalker): Extends the duration of all your dynamic support buffs. The absolute highest throughput multiplier for your team.
  • Haste: Accelerates the cast speeds of your core essence builders and lowers the global cooldown.

Core Buff Engine & Combat Loop

Your mechanical goal is simple: maximize your Ebon Might uptime while executing empowered breath spells to lengthen active team enhancements.

Priority Execution

  • Cast **Ebon Might** instantly to initiate your baseline group enhancement loop before allies hit heavy bursts.
  • Use **Breath of Eons** to lock onto enemy hitboxes, accumulation team damage to explode for massive critical strikes.
  • Cast **Upheaval** at maximum empowerment stacks to knock up targets and lengthen active Ebon Might windows.
  • Execute **Fire Breath** to trigger periodic ticking damage and expand duration metrics.
  • Fill open rotational gaps with **Eruption** to spend active Essence pools and fish for tier procs.
  • Generate necessary resource points using **Living Flame** casts when empty.

Consumables & Enchants

  • Flask: Flask of Tempered Mastery.
  • Weapon Enchant: Enchant Weapon - Authority of the Depths.
  • Food Buff: Feast of Divine Appetites.
