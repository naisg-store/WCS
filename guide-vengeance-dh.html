Vengeance Demon Hunter: Patch 12.0.7 Guide

The Meta Context: Siphoning the Void

Vengeance Demon Hunters excel at dynamic, utility-dense crowd control and baseline magical mitigation. Operating around **Soul Fragments** siphoned directly from enemies, Vengeance tanks bounce between shattering souls for self-healing and hardening their skin with Demon Spikes. Patch 12.0.7 positions them as elite mythic anchors due to their legendary sigil kit (Silence, Misery, and Chains) which controls complex pack positioning single-handedly across the new high-tier *Midnight* instances.

Stat Analysis: Guarding the Frontline

To scale your baseline armor scaling and ensure clean uptime metrics on active mitigations, focus on this distribution:

Agility > Haste > Mastery > Versatility > Critical Strike

  • Agility: Increases baseline attack metrics and converts into passive Dodge value.
  • Haste: The premier secondary stat, drastically lowering the recharge cooldown of your essential Demon Spikes charges.
  • Mastery (Fel Blood): Directly scales the flat defensive armor bonus granted whenever Demon Spikes is active.
  • Versatility: Provides steady, reliable damage reduction and amplifies soul-shattering heals.

Soul Engine Rotation

Your primary goal is to generate Soul Fragments, spend them via Soul Cleave or Spirit Bomb, and manage mitigation charges proactively.

Active Mitigation & Threat Flow

  • Maintain steady charges of **Demon Spikes**, ensuring you never let them cap out during active combat phases.
  • Cast **Fracture** to rapidly slice out Soul Fragments and build initial Fury metrics.
  • Use **Spirit Bomb** at 4-5 Soul Fragments to apply Frailty to all nearby enemies, healing you for a portion of damage dealt.
  • Spend excess Fury pools on **Soul Cleave** for direct frontal recovery and physical target mitigation.
  • Deploy **Fiery Brand** on priority boss targets before heavy mechanical hits arrive to heavily blunt incoming damage.

Consumables & Enhancements

  • Flask: Flask of Alchemical Chaos.
  • Weapon Enchant: Enchant Weapon - Radiant Bulwark / Authority of the Ox.
  • Potions: Algari Healing Potion / Tempered Potion.
