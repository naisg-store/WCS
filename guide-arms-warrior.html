Arms Warrior: Patch 12.0.7 Guide

The Meta Context: Tactician of the Void

In World of Warcraft: Midnight, Arms Warriors represent methodical, crushing impact. Built around deep bleed windows via Deep Wounds and massive tactician resets, Arms focuses on strategic execution rather than breakneck speed. Patch 12.0.7 pathing leans heavily into the Slayer Hero Talent tree, turning Bladelestorm into a premier single-target and priority-cleave execution engine. Don't forget to unlock your **Omnium Folio** passive core runes early in Silvermoon to push your baseline weapon damage to its absolute limit.

Stat Analysis: The Perfect Strike

For Patch 12.0.7, maximizing your Overpower resets and keeping your deep bleeds ticking efficiently dictates this exact priority:

Strength > Critical Strike > Haste > Mastery > Versatility

  • Strength: Boosts baseline attack power and initial damage coefficients.
  • Critical Strike: Essential for triggering massive Impale modifiers and amplifying bleed values.
  • Haste: Speeds up your auto-attack swings for faster rage generation and smooths out the Global Cooldown (GCD).
  • Mastery: Deepens the damage-over-time pressure applied by Deep Wounds.

Core Rotation & Rage Management

Arms is all about pool-and-dump management. Never let your Rage cap, but always ensure you have enough to cast Mortal Strike on cooldown.

Single-Target Priority

  • Apply and maintain **Rend** on the target.
  • Cast **Colossus Smash** (or Avatar combination macro) to trigger your burst window.
  • Use **Mortal Strike** strictly on cooldown to keep Deep Wounds active.
  • Cast **Overpower** whenever available to buff the damage of your next Mortal Strike.
  • Use **Execute** as a primary rage spender whenever the target drops below 20% (or during Sudden Death procs).
  • Fill open gaps with **Slam** only if you are dangerously close to capping Rage.

Consumables & Enchants

  • Flask: Flask of Alchemical Chaos.
  • Weapon Enchant: Enchant Weapon - Authority of the Depths.
  • Food Buff: Feast of Divine Appetites.
