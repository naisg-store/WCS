Fury Warrior: Patch 12.0.7 Guide

The Meta Context: Unbridled Savagery

Fury Warriors remain the premier high-APM, fast-paced choice for melee DPS in Patch 12.0.7. Dual-wielding massive two-handed weapons, Fury centers on maintaining 100% uptime on **Enrage** to shred health bars. In the *Midnight* endgame, Fury stands strong as an A-tier cleave monster for Mythic+ keys due to the sheer consistency of Odyn's Fury and Bloodbath cycles. The addition of the **Omnium Folio** core runes allows Fury to invest points into flat physical penetration, making their constant stream of hits strike even harder.

Stat Analysis: Faster, Harder

Haste is the lifeblood of Fury, dictating your attack speed, rage generation, and spell cooldowns:

Strength > Haste > Mastery > Critical Strike > Versatility

  • Haste: Reduces the global cooldown and speeds up the baseline priority rotation drastically. Aim for your tier thresholds before soft-capping.
  • Mastery (Unshackled Fury): Flatly increases all damage dealt while you are Enraged. Because your uptime should approach 95%+, this stat scales immensely.
  • Critical Strike: Provides regular spikes to your raw ability outcomes and auto-attack generation metrics.

The Enrage Engine Rotation

Your absolute, unchanging goal is to become Enraged and stay Enraged by casting Rampage or triggering specialized procs.

Core Ability Priority

  • Cast **Rampage** immediately as soon as you hit 80+ Rage to trigger or refresh Enrage. Do not overcap.
  • Cast **Avatar** and **Odyn's Fury** on cooldown during burst execution windows.
  • Use **Raging Blow** (or Crushing Blow) to keep your baseline generation flowing.
  • Cast **Bloodthirst** (or Bloodbath) if you need a quick emergency Enrage trigger or health pick-me-up.
  • Use **Execute** whenever a sudden proc occurs or the target passes into the final execution phase.

Consumables & Enhancements

  • Flask: Flask of Alchemical Chaos.
  • Weapon Enchants: Enchant Weapon - Authority of the Depths (Main Hand) / Devotion of Haste (Off-Hand).
  • Combat Potions: Tempered Potion.
